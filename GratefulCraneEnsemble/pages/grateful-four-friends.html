Grateful Four and Friends Variety Show

SHOW TIMES

Sunday | November 19th (Orange County Buddhist Church-MPB) 12 PM Buy Tickets

Description

Please join us for an afternoon filled with dynamic acapella singing, songs from the Yonsei, Sansei and Nisei generations, Taiko, Koto and diverse stories of growing up Japanese American from the Yonsei point of view. The Grateful 4 (Michael Murata, Emily Yoshihara, Lisa Horikawa, Aimee Angeles) is the Grateful Crane Ensemble’s Yonsei Acapella group who have songs to sing, and a wide array of musical talents to share!
Show proceeds will benefit the Grateful Crane Ensemble and OCBC’s Building Fund.

Orange County Buddhist Church
Multipurpose Building (MPB)
909 S Dale Ave, Anaheim, CA, 92804
